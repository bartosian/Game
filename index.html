Rules of the game

You are a spaceship pilot. You must try to destroy all the falling meteorites, while keeping your ship.Each collision of a ship with meteorite will reduce its protection. For each shot down a meteor you will receive points. The game will end when you destroy all meteorites, or lose your ship.

Bonuses in the game

At the beginning of the game you have a certain number of lives and armour, but during the game you can catxh falling bonuses and thereby increase your chances of winning.

Shield, that will add armor to your ship

Ammunition, which at the time will increase the number of shots your ship

Earth satellites that can not be knocked down, since they provide your connection to the planet
